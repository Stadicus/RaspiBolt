[Zap Desktop](https://github.com/LN-Zap/zap-desktop) is a
cross-platform Lightning Network wallet focused on a smooth UX.
Pointing it at your RaspiBolt over gRPC gives you a graphical
desktop wallet that spends and receives using your own channels,
no custodial backend in the middle.
