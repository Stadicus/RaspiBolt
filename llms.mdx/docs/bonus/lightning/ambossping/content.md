AmbossPing is a small bash script that pings
[amboss.space](https://amboss.space) on a schedule to keep your node's
monitoring status fresh. Link your Telegram account to Amboss and
you'll receive push alerts whenever the ping status changes, without
needing ThunderHub or a heavier dashboard.
