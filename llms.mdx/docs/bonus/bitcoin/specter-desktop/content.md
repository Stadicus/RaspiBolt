[Specter Desktop](https://specter.solutions/) wraps Bitcoin Core with
a convenient GUI and is purpose-built around multisignature setups
using hardware wallets and airgapped devices. It's a strong choice
when you want self-custody of larger amounts with a second-factor
signing device.
