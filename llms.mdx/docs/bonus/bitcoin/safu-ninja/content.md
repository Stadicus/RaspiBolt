[Safu Ninja](https://safu.ninja/) is a DIY method for building
fire- and water-resistant steel backups of your mnemonic seed
phrases. It uses only off-the-shelf components, which means no
third-party vendor ever learns you own a hardware wallet or what
the device is guarding.
