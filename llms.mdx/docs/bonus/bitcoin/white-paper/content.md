The Bitcoin white paper was embedded in the blockchain in April 2013
inside block 230,009. This guide walks through extracting and
reassembling the PDF from your own verified blockchain data, a nice
demonstration that the foundational document of Bitcoin is, quite
literally, part of Bitcoin itself.
