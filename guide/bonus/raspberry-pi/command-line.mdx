A small quality-of-life tweak: make your shell prompt shine with a
golden Bitcoin symbol, enable color output for `ls`, and nudge the
defaults so the terminal is easier on the eyes. Purely cosmetic, but
it takes two minutes and you'll appreciate it every time you log
back in.
