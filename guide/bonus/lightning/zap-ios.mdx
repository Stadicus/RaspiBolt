[Zap iOS](https://github.com/LN-Zap/zap-iOS) is an iPhone Lightning
wallet that talks to your own LND node over gRPC. It gives you a
clean mobile UI for managing peers, opening channels, making
payments, and generating invoices, all backed by the node running
in your own home.
