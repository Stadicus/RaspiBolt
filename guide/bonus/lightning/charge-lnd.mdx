[charge-lnd](https://github.com/accumulator/charge-lnd) is a simple,
policy-based fee manager for LND. You write rules matching groups
of channels (by peer, liquidity ratio, or activity) and charge-lnd
applies the matching fee rate on a schedule, so you stop tweaking
channel fees by hand.
